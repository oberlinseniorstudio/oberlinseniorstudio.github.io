Sade with Denim (2018)

Zaid Milefchik

In Sade and Denim a screen shows a text (Sade's 50 Days of Sodom) vertically animated to read, perhaps, how one might read text on an iPhone. This scroll however, moves at a continous pace more like an audiobook. Two 3D objects, photomapped with denim, hover on a layer above the Sade, obscuring the content.
